## Key Concepts to Reference (from public domain summaries)

Lynch's frameworks are widely discussed in investment education:
- **The Six Stock Categories**: Slow growers, stalwarts, fast growers, cyclicals, turnarounds, asset plays
- **The Two-Minute Drill**: Why you own it, what has to happen, what could go wrong
- **PEG Ratio**: Lynch popularized Price/Earnings-to-Growth as a quick valuation check
- **The Cocktail Party Theory**: Four stages of market sentiment
- **"Tenbagger"**: Lynch coined this term for a stock returning 10x
