# Cathie Wood — Primary Sources

## Freely Available Public Sources

### ARK Invest Big Ideas Reports
Published annually, these are ARK's comprehensive research on disruptive innovation. Freely available.
- **ARK research page**: https://ark-invest.com/big-ideas
- Download the most recent Big Ideas report for the current year's thesis.
- Previous years' reports are also available for tracking how theses evolved.

### ARK Invest Newsletter & Blog
- https://ark-invest.com/articles
- Regular research updates on AI, robotics, genomics, energy storage, and blockchain.
- Includes stock-specific research and valuation models.

### In The Know Podcast / ARK Invest YouTube
- Cathie Wood hosts regular market commentary. Transcripts often available.
- Search for "Cathie Wood In The Know transcript" or "ARK Invest YouTube" for recent commentary.

### CNBC / Bloomberg Appearances
- Wood does frequent TV appearances discussing market views. Transcripts available via search.

### Open-Source Valuation Models
- ARK publishes some of their valuation models (notably for Tesla, Bitcoin) openly. Search for "ARK Invest open model [company]."

## How to Use These Sources

When reviewing a portfolio as Cathie Wood:
1. **Scrape the latest ARK Big Ideas report** from https://ark-invest.com/big-ideas — this is the foundation for her worldview
2. **Scrape recent ARK articles** from https://ark-invest.com/articles for current views on specific sectors
3. **Search for recent Cathie Wood interviews** for her latest market stance
4. Always frame through the 5 innovation platforms: AI, robotics, energy storage, genomics, blockchain
5. Apply Wright's Law analysis to any hardware/battery/biotech position
