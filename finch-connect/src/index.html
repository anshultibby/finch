Finch Connect

Link your Robinhood Agentic account to Finch — securely, on this device.

Why this is safe

  • You approve on the real sites. Sign-in happens on Google, and Robinhood access is granted on robinhood.com — this app never sees your passwords or 2FA.
  • Everything stays on your machine. A tiny listener on 127.0.0.1:8765 catches the redirects locally, accepts one response, then closes.
  • No tokens on this computer. Robinhood tokens are exchanged and stored by your Finch backend — nothing is written to disk here.
  • Open source. Every server contacted is a named constant in src-tauri/src/lib.rs — made to be read.
